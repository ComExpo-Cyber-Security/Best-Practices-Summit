About Best Practices Summit

Best Practices Meetup is an exclusive gathering tailored for Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs) and senior cybersecurity professionals, high-level executives and technology leaders including CEOs, CTOs, CIOs, and other decision-makers who are responsible for shaping the strategic direction of their organizations' technology and security frameworks.

What Will Happen?

We will be hosting a series of expert sessions and panel discussions on challenges and issues in FinTech, Healthcare, IT/ITes industries related to cyber security. We'll explore the problems these companies are facing and discover available solutions from cybersecurity companies.

This event provides opportunities for potential startups and organizations to pitch their solutions to CISOs/CXOs in one-to-one networking sessions.

Who Should Attend?

We're specifically inviting FinTech, HealthTech, IT/ITes, SaaS-based product Founders, CXOs, and CISOs from well-known organizations with dedicated cybersecurity budgets. We're also inviting prominent CXOs from the cybersecurity sector to share their knowledge and network with potential clients.

How To Get Here

The event will be held in Gandhinagar, the capital city of Gujarat. Gandhinagar is well-connected by road, rail, and air, making it easily accessible from all major cities in India. The city is known for its wide roads, green spaces, and modern infrastructure.

By Air

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport, Ahmedabad is approximately 30 km from Gandhinagar. Regular flights connect Ahmedabad to all major Indian cities.

Organizers

Learn more about the organizations behind this exclusive summit.

ComExpo Cyber Security Foundation

ComExpo Cyber Security Foundation is a cyber security think tank from Gujarat, a non-profit section-8 organization that believes the web should be safe, open, and equally accessible to everyone.

We enable people from all age groups and professionals to be part of making the web awesome by teaching web literacy skills and spreading awareness about cybersecurity.

With cyber-crime in India increasing by 1500% CAGR, we're working to educate citizens and organizations about cybersecurity best practices. As India revolutionizes FinTech, Smart Cities, Space Technology, and Defence systems, we're helping individuals, companies, and startups strengthen their security posture.

The Hackers Meetup

The Hackers Meetup is a monthly must-attend event that features in the calendars of security researchers, hackers, and professionals around the nation.

The event brings together security researchers, hackers, business leaders, entrepreneurs, practitioners from academia, industry, and government organizations, as well as students to discuss IT security challenges.

It's not just an event, but a crucial opportunity for top-level executives to stay ahead in the ever-evolving landscape of cybersecurity, enhance knowledge, expand professional networks, and lead organizations with confidence in the digital age.
